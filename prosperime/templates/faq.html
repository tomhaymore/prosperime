Frequently Asked Questions

Q:Why Join?
To help out kids who were just like you, bright, driven students who may have needed a little help finding their way. Whether you realize it or not, you've collected a wealth of information and experience during your career that could be invaluable to a young professional who is just starting out. Sharing this information with them could mean all the difference in their lives, and help guide them toward a profession that really love.

If you aren't convinced yet, here are some other great reasons to take part:
  1. Show yourself as an industry expert,
  2. Represent your company, and
  3. Develop valuable relationships with motivated, talented students.
Q:How does it work?
Emplore works by creating tight-knit conversations around students' burning career questions. We emphasize tight-knit, as we place heavy emphasis on finding the right people for each conversation; if your background is in Finance, we won't bother your with questions about Medical School.
Q:I'm worried about time commitment
Trust, me understand. Our #1 priority is not to waste anyone's time. We work to ensure this in a few ways:
  1. You will not be asked to join a conversation until the conversation has met a minimum threshold of traction among students, represented by users "following" your conversation. That way we ensure that you have an audience, and that your question is meaningful to students.
  2. You dictate which conversations you want to be a part of. You can answer at your convenience--once a week or once a month, it's completely up to you.
  3. There is no contract of mentorship involved, and your interactions are not limited to just one student. We know that one-on-one mentoring can be a terrific resource, but we also understand the burden that comes with that commitment. On Emplore, a single answer can serve hundreds of students instantly, and you don't have to worry about scheduling a second coffee date next week.
Q:How can I get more involved?
On the flipside, if you become a part of a great conversation or want to engage with more students, let us know! We can work toward creating the conversations that you are interested in, and target the most relevant students who would benefit from your participation.
